# 📊 Customer Feedback Insights

## Key Topics Identified

### Topic 1: Delivery & Speed
- Keywords: delivery, fast, delayed
- Insight: Customers frequently mention delivery times.

### Topic 2: Customer Service
- Keywords: service, support, help
- Insight: Customer service quality is inconsistent.

### Topic 3: Product Quality & Pricing
- Keywords: quality, price, expensive
- Insight: Customers care about value for money.

## Key Observations
- Delivery speed is a major driver of satisfaction.
- Poor service experiences negatively impact perception.
- Pricing concerns appear with product quality feedback.

## Recommendations
- 🚚 Improve delivery timelines and logistics.
- 📞 Train customer service teams for better support.
- 💰 Review pricing strategy to align with perceived value.
